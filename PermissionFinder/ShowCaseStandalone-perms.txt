---------- Permission nodes for ShowCaseStandalone ----------
scs.use - Gives players permission to use SCS
scs.create.buy - Gives players permission to create buy showcases
scs.create.sell - Gives players permission to create sell showcases
scs.create.display - Gives players permission to create display showcases
scs.remove - Gives players permission to remove showcases
scs.manage - Gives player ability to manage showcases
scs.create - Gives players permission to create any kind of scs
scs.create.unlimited - Gives player ability to create unlimited showcases
scs.admin - Gives player ability to administrate showcases
scs.* - Gives all permissions for ShowcaseStandalone
