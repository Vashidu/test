---------- Permission nodes for AlphaChest ----------
alphachest.* - Gives access to the virtual chest and workbench.
alphachest.chest - Gives access to the virtual chest.
alphachest.workbench - Gives access to the virtual workbench.
alphachest.keepOnDeath - Allows to keep the chest contents on death even if clearOnDeath setting is enabled.
alphachest.admin - Allows to open and clear someone else's chest.
alphachest.save - Allows to save all virtual chests.
